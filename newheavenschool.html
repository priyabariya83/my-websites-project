About New Heaven School

New Heaven School, Waghodia, Vadodara, offers Nursery to 12th education in both Gujarati and English mediums. We promote academic excellence, values, discipline, and creativity in every student.

Smart classrooms, co-curriculars, and dedicated staff help us shape future leaders with confidence and compassion.
