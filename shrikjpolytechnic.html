College Overview

Shri K. J. Polytechnic, Bharuch was established in 2013 and is affiliated to Gujarat Technological University (GTU). It is approved by AICTE and offers diploma programs across various engineering streams.

With strong industry tie-ups, great infrastructure, and a focus on practical learning, the college empowers students for careers in both the private and public sectors.

Admission Process

Admissions are conducted as per Gujarat Technological University norms. Students must apply through the official government counseling process based on their 10th standard marks.

Required Documents:

  • 10th Marksheet
  • School Leaving Certificate
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport Size Photos
